About Us

Anthony Mendoza:
Built for the Working Class

Triple A&C Automotive is owned and operated by Anthony Mendoza, a skilled technician who built this shop on a simple principle: quality auto repair shouldn't break the bank. Located in Buda, TX and serving the greater Kyle and Hays County area, we deliver dealer-quality repairs at prices real people can afford.

We believe in honesty and integrity above all. That means clear, upfront pricing with no hidden fees, repairs you actually need, and communication throughout the entire process. Every customer gets treated with respect and transparency.

From a quick oil change to diesel repair, A/C service, brake jobs, engine diagnostics, and suspension work — our experienced mechanics handle it all. We work on all makes and models.
